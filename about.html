History

Open since 1938, the Chalet Motel has been a landmark "home away from home" for visitors to the Black Hills for more than 80 years. Originally named Ben's Cabins, it was constructed as a cabin court or motor court. The Chalet Motel has had 5 owners and was purchased in late 2018 by a local 25-year hotel executive. We are open seasonally from May through October.

Having been in the hospitality industry for more than 25 years, we have it down. Our staff are locals who can share insight and information to help you explore the area and find hidden treasures. Please contact us, even before you come to the area, to see if we can assist you with planning your trip to Custer.
